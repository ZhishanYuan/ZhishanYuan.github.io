Hi, everyone! My name is Zhishan Yuan. I am a first-year PhD student in Computer Science at Stevens Institute of Technology.

My research interests include natural language processing, large language models, medical AI, and model interpretability. I am particularly interested in understanding and improving the reasoning behavior of large language models in expert domains.

Before joining Stevens, I studied Artificial Intelligence at Huazhong University of Science and Technology. During my undergraduate study, I was honored as an Outstanding Undergraduate in Academic Performance. I also attended the Data Science Summer School at Imperial College London in 2024, where I worked on brain tumor segmentation based on U-Net.

I previously worked as a research intern at Lenovo, Shanghai, focusing on evaluating large language models across different datasets.
